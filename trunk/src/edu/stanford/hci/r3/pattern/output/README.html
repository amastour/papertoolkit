Printing Pattern

PDFPatternGenerator can create PDF files with embedded pattern. We have tested the printing the following printers and fonts:

HP LaserJet 4250 PS

The pen buzzes while streaming, but it works with these fonts, sizes, and resolutions:

  • Tahoma 20 w/ ProRes 1200 but NOT with 600dpi
  • Tahoma 21 w/ 600dpi
  • Tahoma 23 w/ 600dpi and ProRes 1200
  • Tahoma 25 w/ 600dpi and ProRes 1200 (at 600dpi it looks too small to the eye)
  • Tahoma 26 w/ ProRes1200
  • Tahoma 27 w/ 600dpi, ProRes1200
  • Tahoma 28 w/ ProRes 1200 also (but the dots seem too big to the eye), w/ 600dpi
  • Tahoma 29 w/ 600dpi (but dots seem too dark)
  • Tahoma 30 w/ 600dpi (but dots seem too dark)
You may have to customize it to work with your own printer.
